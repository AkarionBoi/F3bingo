Fallout 3 Bingo Board version 1.0 by Kungkobra, Akarion, Radioactiv03.

1. open up f3.htm in your favourite Netscape alternative
2. generate a cool card that you like
3. if you have any friends (haha yeah right) copy the seed number at the end of the URL and share it
4. try to find the fastest row/column and complete it
5. do with or without glitches, idc

If you want more goals, open f3.js in the folder and add them accordingly.
Earlier goals are easier/short ones, later ones are harder/longer ones, on a scale of 1 to 25.
